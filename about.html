Hwa Ran — Seoul, Korea

Tattoo Artist

Drawing
with Care

Based in Korea, I focus on delicate and expressive compositions inspired by nature and oriental aesthetics.

Specialising in oriental and floral designs, I aim to create work that feels calm, balanced, and deeply connected to the flow of the body.

I believe drawing is not just a step before tattooing, but an essential part of the entire process. Each line should be placed with intention and care, allowing the design to naturally grow from observation and patience.

This philosophy is also reflected in my approach to tattooing — slow, thoughtful, and respectful of both the subject and the skin.
